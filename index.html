About

Most IT professionals have encountered a paradox when companies are continuously rejecting their applications due to a lack of experience. When you try to gain experience, you are rejected because you don't have experience.

Here steps in WisePace, a mentorship program that helps aspiring IT professionals land their dream jobs in just a few months. We offer a unique learning approach that accelerates your journey into the IT industry, providing you with the skills and experience needed to succeed.

Our results are guaranteed by the students who already have successfully entered the job market after completing our learning program.

Through hands-on, project-based learning, we equip students and junior developers with real-world experience, fostering skills that make them competitive in today's fast-paced tech landscape.

Our process

1. Initial consultation (~45min)

We take a call with you to discuss your existing skills, identify areas for improvement, and explain how we can help enhance your abilities. We'll also discuss how we can offer such a fast way of learning!

2. Contract signing (~30min)

We sign a contract with you as soon as you're ready. We give you time to think, but no more than one week.

3. General onboarding (1-2h)

A comprehensive onboarding to our technical stack and project. We provide insights about the project, introduce you to our internal development team, and walk you through our workflows.

4. Learning path (6-12 months)

The learning path takes up to 6 months, depending on your skills. We strive to get you to the job market as fast as possible. The more skills and motivation you have, the faster the results.

5. Long-awaited success!

Enjoy the job market and the possibility to work for your first IT company!
