About Wisepace

Wisepace connects passionate IT learners with forward-thinking companies through our unique work-based learning approach. We combine hands-on experience with real cloud projects and expert mentorship to create job-ready developers.

For learners, this means gaining practical skills and industry experience from day one. For companies, this means access to a pool of talented developers who are ready to increase your revenue growth and drive innovation.

We train our students to tackle AI tools effectively while maintaining critical thinking skills. We create developers who know when to leverage AI and when to rely on their expertise.

Our Mission

We're transforming the IT job market by making it more welcoming for newcomers while connecting companies with business-minded tech talents who drive real growth.

Whether you're a student, junior developer, or career changer, we'll help you build confidence and skills through hands-on experience. For companies, we're your partner in finding exceptional tech talent that understands both code and business value.

Our Vision

We're building Estonia's future as a global hub for IT talent and education, where geographical boundaries no longer limit your career growth.

As a fully remote academy, we're pioneering sustainable IT education that lets you learn from anywhere - whether it's a beach in Tenerife or a cozy cafe in the Baltics.

By eliminating traditional office spaces, we're not just reducing our environmental impact - we're creating a borderless learning experience that adapts to your lifestyle while maintaining the highest standards of IT education.
